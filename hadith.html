Sahih al-Bukhari #7121 Kitab al-Fitan
لا تقوم الساعة حتى يقتتل فئتان عظيمتان تكون بينهما مقتلة عظيمة دعواهما واحدة

"The Hour will not be established until two large groups fight each other, resulting in great casualties, while their claim is one and the same."

Scholarly Consensus: Classical scholars like Al-Qurtubi and Ibn Hajar refer this to the Battle of Siffin.
