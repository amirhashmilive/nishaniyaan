Phase 5 — Heavenly Intervention

Phase 5: Descent of Isa (AS)

Prophet Jesus (*Isa ibn Maryam, peace be upon him*) descends from heaven to slay Dajjal, break false doctrines, and establish divine truth.

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an 4:159) 🟢 Sahih Muslim Verified

Phase 5 — Descent Location

Descent at the White Minaret in Damascus

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Supported by Two Angels

Isa (AS) descends during Fajr prayer at the eastern white minaret of Damascus, wearing light saffron robes, resting his hands on the wings of two angels.

Phase 5 — Physical Attributes

Physical Appearance & Noble Demeanor

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #3448

The True Messiah's Attributes

Well-proportioned height, reddish-white skin, straight dark hair dripping as if fresh from a bath. When he lowers his head, beads like silver pearls drop from it.

Phase 5 — Global Mission

Breaking the Cross & Abolishing Jizyah

🟢 Surah An-Nisa (4:159) 🟢 Sahih Bukhari #2222

He will break the cross, slay the swine, and abolish the *Jizyah* tax — as all remaining people of the Scripture will recognize his truth and accept pure monotheism (*Tawhid*).

Phase 5 — Destruction of Falsehood

Slaying Dajjal at Bab al-Ludd (Lydda)

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

The End of the Deceiver

Isa (AS) pursues Dajjal to the gate of Ludd (Lydda, near Jerusalem). Upon seeing Isa (AS), Dajjal begins dissolving like salt in water. Isa (AS) overtakes him and strikes him down with his spear.

🟢 Sahih Hadith Verified

Victory of Divine Truth Over Falsehood

Dajjal is vanquished forever, his deceptive spell broken. The believers rejoice as peace and divine law spread across every corner of the earth.
