Chapter 06 — Slide 01

Major Sign 01: Al-Masih ad-Dajjal

SIGN-MAJ-001

The False Messiah & Greatest Tribulation

The greatest trial from Creation to Qiyamah. A one-eyed deceiver who will claim divinity, perform supernatural feats, and lead many astray until defeated by Isa (AS) at the Gate of Ludd.

Chapter 06 — Slide 02

Major Signs 02 & 03: Mahdi & Descent of Isa (AS)

SIGN-MAJ-002

Imam Al-Mahdi

Righteous leader from the Prophet's lineage filling the earth with justice.

SIGN-MAJ-003

Descent of Isa (AS)

Descent at Damascus white minaret to slay Dajjal and establish Islamic law.

Chapter 06 — Slide 03

Major Sign 04: Yajuj & Majuj (Gog & Magog)

SIGN-MAJ-004

The Swarming Tribes

Vast tribes currently restrained behind the barrier built by Dhul-Qarnayn who will break through and swarm down from every elevation until destroyed by divine intervention.
