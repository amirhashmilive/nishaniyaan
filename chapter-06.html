Phase 5 — Ultimate Victory

Phase 5: Descent of Isa (AS)

The return of Prophet Jesus (*Isa ibn Maryam*) from heaven to slay Dajjal and restore divine truth.

Phase 5 — Heavenly Return

Descent at the White Minaret in Damascus

Sahih Muslim #2937

Supported by Two Angels

Isa (AS) descends at the eastern white minaret of Damascus, wearing garments lightly saffron-dyed, resting his hands on the wings of two angels.

Phase 5 — Physical Attributes

Physical Description & Demeanor

Sahih Bukhari #3448

Appearance of the True Messiah

Moderate height, reddish-white complexion, straight hair dripping as if fresh from a bath. When he lowers his head, beads like pearls fall.

Phase 5 — Universal Mission

Ending Falsehood & Establishing Law

Sahih Bukhari #2222

A Just Ruler Under Islamic Shari'ah

He will break the cross, abolish the *Jizyah* tax (as all accept truth), and rule strictly according to the revelation of Muhammad ﷺ.

Phase 5 — Defeat of Falsehood

Slaying Dajjal at Bab al-Ludd (Lydda)

Sahih Muslim #2937

The End of the False Messiah

Isa (AS) pursues Dajjal to the gate of Ludd (near modern Lydda/Ben Gurion). Upon seeing Isa (AS), Dajjal begins to dissolve like salt in water until Isa (AS) strikes him down with his spear.

Victory of Truth Over Falsehood

With Dajjal slain, deception vanishes completely. The believers rejoice as truth permanently triumphs over falsehood.
