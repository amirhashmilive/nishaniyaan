Phase 5 — Divine Return

Phase 5: Descent of Prophet Isa (AS)

The return of Prophet Jesus, Son of Mary (*Isa ibn Maryam*), descending physically from the heavens to destroy Dajjal, abolish falsehood, and establish righteous rule.

🟢 Qur'anic & Hadith Authenticated

Phase 5 — The Miracle of Descent

Descent at the White Minaret of Damascus

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Isa (AS) will descend at the white minaret east of Damascus, wearing two lightly dyed garments, resting his hands on the wings of two angels. When he lowers his head, beads of perspiration fall like pearls; when he raises it, drops like pearls scatter.

Phase 5 — Physical Appearance

Prophetic Appearance of Isa (AS)

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #3438

A man of medium height, reddish-white complexion, broad chest, with straight, flowing hair as if he had just emerged from a hot bath (*Deemas*).

Phase 5 — Abolition of Falsehood

Breaking the Cross & Ending Jizya

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #2222

"By Him in Whose Hand is my soul, the Son of Mary will soon descend among you as a just judge. He will break the cross, kill the swine, and abolish the Jizya tax." All religious rivalries vanish, leaving Islam as the sole faith.

Phase 5 — Termination of the Deceiver

The Elimination of Dajjal at Bab al-Ludd

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Dissolution at Lydda

Isa (AS) pursues Dajjal to the Gate of Ludd (Lod, Palestine). As soon as Dajjal catches sight of Isa (AS), he begins dissolving like salt in water. Isa strikes him with his spear, displaying his blood on the spearhead.

🟢 Surah Al-Isra (17:81)

وَقُلْ جَآءَ ٱلْحَقُّ وَزَهَقَ ٱلْبَـٰطِلُ ۚ إِنَّ ٱلْبَـٰطِلَ كَانَ زَهُوقًا

Victory of Truth Over Falsehood

"And say: Truth has come and falsehood has vanished. Indeed, falsehood is ever bound to vanish."
