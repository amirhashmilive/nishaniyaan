Mission & Editorial Standards

About Nishaniyaan (نشانیاں)

Preserving scholarly eschatological knowledge with clarity, reverence, and evidence.

Core Philosophy & Scholarly Code

  1. Qur'an as Highest Authority: Presented cleanly without conflating divine text with human opinion.
  2. Hadith Categorized by Authenticity: Explicit visual badges for Sahih, Hasan, Da'if, and Mawdu'.
  3. Strict Separation of Sources: Distinct demarcation between Qur'an, Authentic Sunnah, Classical Tafsir, Historical Evidence, and Modern Observations.
  4. No Sensational Speculation: Contemporary events are treated as non-binding observations unless supported by clear text and scholarly consensus.
