Chapter 03 — Slide 01

Sahih al-Bukhari & Proximity of the Hour

Sahih al-Bukhari #4936 Agreed Upon
بُعِثْتُ أَنَا وَالسَّاعَةَ كَهَاتَيْنِ

"I have been sent and the Hour is like these two," holding up his index and middle fingers together to illustrate proximity.

Chapter 03 — Slide 02

Sahih Muslim & The Supreme Tribulation

Sahih Muslim #2937 Kitab al-Fitan
مَا بَيْنَ خَلْقِ آدَمَ إِلَى قِيَامِ السَّاعَةِ خَلْقٌ أَكْبَرُ مِنَ الدَّجَّالِ

"There is no creation between the creation of Adam and the onset of the Hour greater than Dajjal."
