Chapter 03 — Proximity of the Hour

Sahih al-Bukhari #4936

🟢 Sahih — Agreed Upon

بُعِثْتُ أَنَا وَالسَّاعَةَ كَهَاتَيْنِ

"I have been sent and the Hour is like these two," — holding up his index and middle fingers together to illustrate their proximity.

Chapter 03 — The Supreme Tribulation

Sahih Muslim #2937

🟢 Sahih Muslim — Kitab al-Fitan

مَا بَيْنَ خَلْقِ آدَمَ إِلَى قِيَامِ السَّاعَةِ خَلْقٌ أَكْبَرُ مِنَ الدَّجَّالِ

"There is no creation between the creation of Adam and the onset of the Hour that is greater in tribulation than al-Dajjal."
