Phase 2 — Immediate Precursors

Phase 2: Just Before Dajjal (Near Signs)

These are the immediate cataclysmic precursors signaling the imminent arrival of Al-Masih ad-Dajjal. They unfold rapidly, shaking planetary stability.

🟢 Sahih Hadith Verified 🟢 Authentic (Qur'an)

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 01

The Three Great Landslides (*Khusuf*)

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2901

Global Tectonic Cave-Ins

Three massive land cave-ins of unprecedented scale:

  • Landslide in the East: Massive faultline failure in Asia/East.
  • Landslide in the West: Tectonic devastation in Western Americas/Europe.
  • Landslide in the Arabian Peninsula: Structural collapse across Arabia.

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 02

The Smoke (*Ad-Dukhan*)

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) 🟢 Sahih Muslim
Surah Ad-Dukhan (44:10-11)

فَارْتَقِبْ يَوْمَ تَأْتِي السَّمَاءُ بِدُخَانٍ مُّبِينٍ ﴿١٠﴾ يَغْشَى النَّاسَ هَٰذَا عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ ﴿١١﴾

"Then watch for the Day when the sky will bring a visible smoke, enveloping the people; this is a painful torment."

Atmospheric Effect:

A thick haze covering earth for 40 days — affecting believers like a mild cold, while disbelievers suffer severe pressure.

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 03

The Beast of the Earth (*Dabbat al-Ard*)

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) 🟢 Sahih Muslim
Surah An-Naml (27:82)

وَإِذَا وَقَعَ الْقَوْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ أَخْرَجْنَا لَهُمْ دَابَّةً مِّنَ الْأَرْضِ تُكَلِّمُهُمْ...

"And when the word befalls them, We will bring forth for them a creature from the earth speaking to them..."

Distinguishing Mark:

Carrying the Staff of Musa (AS) and Ring of Sulayman (AS), marking the faces of believers and disbelievers explicitly.

Phase 2 — Cosmic Shift

Sun Rising from the West

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) 🟢 Sahih Bukhari #4635
Surah Al-An'am (6:158) & Sahih Tradition

The sun rises from its place of setting in the West. Upon witnessing this cosmic reversal, all humanity will believe — but the door of repentance (*Tawbah*) will be permanently closed.

Phase 2 — Migration Force

The Fire Gathering Humanity (*Nar al-Yaman*)

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2901

Fire Emerging from Aden

A massive volcanic fire emerges from the bottom of Aden (Yemen), driving people forcefully from all regions toward the gathering place (*Mahshar*) in Sham (Greater Syria).

Phase 2 — Geopolitics

The Great Battle (*Al-Malhama al-Kubra*)

🟢 Sahih Abu Dawud #4294

Global Armageddon Near Dabiq

An unprecedented military confrontation occurring near Dabiq/Amaq in Syria between Muslim forces and alliance armies, resulting in severe casualties immediately preceding Dajjal's emergence.

🟢 Sahih Muslim

Collapse of Empires & 3 Famine Years

The existing geopolitical order collapses under three consecutive years of catastrophic drought and global food scarcity, creating widespread desperation that Dajjal exploits upon his arrival.
