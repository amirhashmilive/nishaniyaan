Phase 2 — Immediate Precursors

Phase 2: Just Before Dajjal (Near Signs)

Cataclysmic signs and cosmic events occurring in rapid succession immediately prior to Dajjal's appearance.

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 01

The Three Great Landslides (*Khusuf*)

SIGN-MAJ-008 · Sahih Muslim

Major Earth Cave-Ins

Three massive land cave-ins: one in the East, one in the West, and one in the Arabian Peninsula.

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 02

The Smoke (*Ad-Dukhan*)

Surah Ad-Dukhan (44:10-11)

Atmospheric Haze Covering Earth

A thick smoke enveloping humanity for 40 days, causing a cold-like illness in believers and severe agony for disbelievers.

Phase 2 — Cataclysm 03

The Beast of the Earth (*Dabbat al-Ard*)

Surah An-Naml (27:82)

Emergence of the Beast

A unique creature emerging to speak to people and mark their faces, distinguishing believers from disbelievers.

Phase 2 — Cosmic Shift

Sun Rising from the West

SIGN-MAJ-007 · Sahih Bukhari

Closing of the Door of Repentance

The sun rises from its place of setting. Once witnessed, faith will no longer benefit any soul that did not believe prior.

Phase 2 — Migration Force

The Fire Gathering Humanity

SIGN-MAJ-009 · Sahih Muslim

Fire from Yemen

A great fire emerging from the bottom of Aden (Yemen), driving people from all corners of the earth toward Sham (Syria).

Phase 2 — Geopolitics

The Great Battle (*Al-Malhama al-Kubra*)

SIGN-MAJ-010 · Sahih Muslim

Global Geopolitical Conflict

A colossal military conflict around Dabiq/Amaq preceding the emergence of Dajjal during extreme economic famine.

Collapse of Empires & Famine

Three years of severe drought and global economic failure immediately precede the appearance of Dajjal, creating desperation among the masses.
