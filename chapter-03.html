Phase 2 — Seismic Sinking

The Three Great Landslides (*Khusuf*)

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2901

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ enumerated three monumental land collapses (*Khusuf*) where massive regions of the Earth will sink into subterranean chasms:

1. Landslide in East

Sinking of major eastern landmasses.

2. Landslide in West

Sinking of western continental zones.

3. Arabian Peninsula

Sinking within the heart of Arabia.

Phase 3 — Atmospheric Cloud

The Smoke (*Ad-Dukhan*)

🟢 Surah Ad-Dukhan (44:10-11) 🟢 Sahih Muslim

فَٱرْتَقِبْ يَوْمَ تَأْتِى ٱلسَّمَآءُ بِدُخَانٍ مُّبِينٍ

"Then watch for the Day when the sky will bring a visible smoke, enveloping the people; this is a painful torment."

A dense, literal smoke covering the entire globe for 40 days and nights. Believers experience a mild cold-like congestion while disbelievers suffer severe pressure.

Phase 2 — Miraculous Emergence

The Beast of the Earth (*Dabbat al-Ard*)

🟢 Surah An-Naml (27:82) 🟢 Sahih Muslim

A miraculous creature emerging from the earth holding the Staff of Musa (AS) and the Ring of Sulaiman (AS). It speaks to mankind, placing a glowing mark on believers' faces and a dark seal on disbelievers' noses.

Phase 2 — Cosmic Inversion

The Sun Rising from the West

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #4635 & Sahih Muslim #157

Closure of the Door of Repentance (*Tawbah*)

The rotation or cosmic orientation of the Earth alters, causing the Sun to rise from its place of setting in the West. At that instant, the door of divine repentance is permanently sealed.

Phase 2 — Gathering Fire

The Great Fire of Yemen

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2901

A colossal fire emerging from the pit of Aden in Yemen, driving humanity toward the land of gathering (*Mahshar* in Syria/Levant), resting with them when they rest and moving when they move.

Great Wars & The Battle of Al-Malhamah

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2897

The Great War (*Al-Malhamah al-Kubra*) fought near Dabiq/Amiq in Syria. Unprecedented casualties occurs prior to Constantinople's bloodless conquest through Takbir (*Allah-u-Akbar*).

Phase 2 — World Order Vacuum

Collapse of Global Empires & Systems

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2882 🟡 Hasan Narration

Collapse of secular global hegemony and trade networks. Immediate precursors include the swallowing of Sufyani's hostile army in the desert of Al-Bayda between Makkah and Madinah, alongside double eclipses during Ramadan and a long-tailed comet rising in the East.
