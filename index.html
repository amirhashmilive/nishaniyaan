Frequently Asked Questions

How does Nishaniyaan ensure scholarly integrity?
Every claim on Nishaniyaan strictly separates Qur'an, Sahih Hadith, scholarly consensus, historical events, and modern observations. Contemporary events are labeled strictly as observations—never definitively declared as fulfilled prophecies without scholarly consensus.
How are unverified reports and modern rumors handled?
Unverified news items or internet rumors are quarantined under our dedicated **Research & Unverified Section** with prominent black badges (⚫ Unverified Report) to explicitly warn users against weak or fabricated attributions.
