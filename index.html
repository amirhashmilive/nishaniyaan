1. Imam al-Mahdi

🟢 Sahih Hadith
  • Appears first during widespread injustice, tyranny, and severe turmoil
  • Muslims pledge allegiance to him between the Corner and the Maqam in Makkah
  • Leads the Muslim nation with righteousness and establishes global justice

2. Al-Masih ad-Dajjal

🟢 Sahih Hadith
  • Emerges during the era of Imam al-Mahdi from the East
  • Claims extraordinary powers, prophethood, and eventually divinity
  • His trial (fitnah) is the greatest test for humanity from Adam to Qiyamah

3. Isa ibn Maryam (AS)

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) 🟢 Sahih Hadith
  • Descends from heaven near the white minaret east of Damascus
  • Prays behind Imam al-Mahdi in Jerusalem (Al-Quds)
  • Pursues Al-Masih ad-Dajjal and kills him at the Gate of Ludd (Lod)
