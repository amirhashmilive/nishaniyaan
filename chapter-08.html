Phase 7 — The Final Countdown

Phase 7: After Dajjal to Qiyamah

The gentle breeze taking the souls of believers, followed by cosmic destruction, Israfil's horn blasts, and universal resurrection.

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an 39:68) 🟢 Sahih Muslim Verified

Phase 7 — Cosmic Death

The First Trumpet Blast (*Nafkhat al-Sa'aq*)

🟢 Surah Az-Zumar (39:68)

Universal Swooning & Cosmic Destruction

Israfil (AS) blows the Trumpet, causing all living creatures in the heavens and earth to fall dead instantly, except those whom Allah wills to spare. Mountains crumble into dust.

Phase 7 — Universal Rebirth

The Second Trumpet Blast & Resurrection

🟢 Surah Yasin (36:51)

Rising from the Graves (*Nafkhat al-Ba'th*)

The Trumpet is sounded a second time, and behold — all of humanity rises from their graves, regenerated from the coccyx bone (*'Ajb al-Dhanab*), hastening toward their Creator.

Phase 7 — Final Signs

The Fire Gathering Humanity to Mahshar

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2901

The Last Physical Sign Before Judgment

A volcanic fire emerging from Yemen pushes remaining people across the earth toward the Plain of Reckoning (*Mahshar*) in Sham, staying with them as they rest and move.

🟢 Surah Al-Haqqah (69:13-18)

The End of the World — Eternal Reckoning

The physical universe is dissolved and replaced with the eternal realm. Humanity stands exposed before Allah Al-Wahid Al-Qahhar for the weighing of deeds (*Mizan*) and crossing the Sirat.
