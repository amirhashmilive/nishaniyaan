Phase 7 — Departure of Believers

The Gentle Breeze (*Rih Layyinah*)

🟢 Sahih Muslim #117

Allah sends a sweet, soothing wind from Yemen softer than silk. It touches everyone who has even a atom's weight of faith in their heart, taking their souls peacefully. Only the worst of creation remain to witness the final terror of Qiyamah.

Phase 7 — Cosmic Termination

The First Trumpet Blast (*Nafkhat as-Sa'iq*)

🟢 Surah Az-Zumar (39:68) 🟢 Sahih Bukhari

وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَصَعِقَ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَمَن فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ إِلَّا مَن شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ

"And the Trumpet will be blown, and whoever is in the heavens and whoever is on the earth will fall dead except whom Allah wills."

Phase 7 — Universal Resurrection

The Second Trumpet Blast (*Nafkhat al-Ba'th*)

🟢 Surah Az-Zumar (39:68) 🟢 Sahih Muslim

ثُمَّ نُفِخَ فِيهِ أُخْرَىٰ فَإِذَا هُمْ قِيَامٌ يَنظُرُونَ

"Then it will be blown a second time, and at once they will be standing, looking on."

Rain falls from beneath the Throne for 40 periods, causing bodies to grow from their tailbones (*Ajb adh-Dhanab*) before the soul is reunited.

Phase 7 — Concluding Events

Destruction of Kaaba & Removal of Qur'an

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #1591 & Sahih Muslim

Destruction of the Kaaba by Dhu al-Suwayqatayn from Abyssinia; lifting of the Holy Qur'an from pages and hearts overnight; the Gathering (*Al-Hashr*) on the bare, reddish-white plain of Resurrection.
