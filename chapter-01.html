Prophetic Lineage & Warnings

Human History: From Adam to Muhammad ﷺ

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an Verified)
Adam (AS): First prophet, created by Allah, granted knowledge of all names.
Nuh (AS): Warned his nation for 950 years; built the Ark before the Great Flood.
Ibrahim (AS): Patriarch of prophets (*Khalilullah*); rebuilt the Holy Kaaba.
Musa (AS): Received the Torah (*Tawrat*); liberated Bani Israel from Pharaoh.
Isa (AS): Born of Virgin Maryam (*Gospel/Injeel*); raised to heaven, returning in End Times.
Muhammad ﷺ: Seal of the Prophets; revealed the final Qur'an & warned of Dajjal.

Key Message: Every single prophet explicitly warned their nation about the end times and the supreme trial of the False Messiah (*Dajjal*).
