The Period of Al-Masih ad-Dajjal

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ warned that Dajjal will remain on earth for 40 days: the 1st day like a year (360 days), the 2nd like a month (30 days), the 3rd like a week (7 days), and the remaining 37 days as normal days. This totals 434–439 days of cosmic and spiritual trial before his defeat by Isa (AS).
