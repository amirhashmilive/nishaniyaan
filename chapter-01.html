Introduction & Project Scope

Objective & Core Philosophy

Nishaniyaan is a scholarly reference archive cataloging the Signs of the Day of Judgment (*Ashrat as-Sa'ah*). It upholds strict academic rigor by separating Qur'an, authentic Hadith, classical commentary, and modern observations.

Qur'an as Highest Authority

Presented unmixed with human interpretation.

Hadith Verification

Categorized strictly by authenticity grade.

Quarantined Warning Archive

Isolates unverified claims from authentic text.

The 9 Visual Verification Levels

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an)

Explicit Divine Verse

Direct text from the Holy Qur'an.

🟢 Sahih Hadith

Authentic Prophetic Text

Bukhari, Muslim, and verified Sahih Sunan.

🟡 Hasan Hadith

Acceptable Narration

Good chain of transmission accepted for context.

⚪ Scholarly Opinion

Classical Commentary

Ibn Kathir, Al-Qurtubi, Ibn Hajar exegesis.

🟣 Contemporary Observation

Modern Phenomena

Trends observed in modern society.

⚫ Unverified Report

Quarantined Rumor

Isolated weak or fabricated claims.
