Chapter 01

Introduction & Project Scope

Nishaniyaan is a scholarly reference archive cataloging the Signs of the Day of Judgment. It upholds strict academic rigor by separating Qur'an, authentic Hadith, classical commentary, and modern observations.

Chapter 01 — Core Philosophy

Three Foundational Principles

Qur'an as Highest Authority

Presented separately from human interpretation. The divine text stands alone.

Hadith Graded by Authenticity

Sahih, Hasan, Da'if, and Mawdu' — each clearly labeled with full chain references.

Quarantined Warning Archive

Unverified claims are isolated in a dedicated section — never mixed with authentic material.

Chapter 01 — Verification System

The 9 Visual Verification Levels

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an)

Explicit divine verses.

🟢 Sahih Hadith

Bukhari, Muslim, verified Sunan.

🟡 Hasan

Good chain, accepted for context.

🟠 Da'if (Weak)

Deficient chain, noted only.

🔴 Mawdu' (Fabricated)

Rejected, quarantined.

⚪ Scholarly Opinion

Classical commentaries.

🔵 Historical Record

Documented events.

🟣 Observation

Modern phenomena noted, not confirmed.

⚫ Unverified

Internet rumors, quarantined.
