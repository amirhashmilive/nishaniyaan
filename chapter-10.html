Prophetic Sunnah

Authentic Hadith Collections

Canonical Compendiums: Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abi Dawud, Tirmidhi, and Ibn Majah.

Sahih al-Bukhari

Kitab al-Fitan (Book of Trials)

Sahih al-Bukhari #7118

لاَ تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى تَخْرُجَ نَارٌ مِنْ أَرْضِ الْحِجَازِ

"The Hour will not come until a fire emerges from the land of the Hijaz illuminating the necks of camels in Busra."

Sahih Muslim

Kitab al-Fitan (Book of Tribulations)

Sahih Muslim #2937

مَا بَيْنَ خَلْقِ آدَمَ إِلَى قِيَامِ السَّاعَةِ خَلْقٌ أَكْبَرُ مِنَ الدَّجَّالِ

"There is no creation between the creation of Adam and the onset of the Hour greater than Dajjal."

Sunan Abi Dawud

Kitab al-Malahim (Book of Battles)

Sunan Abi Dawud #4286

الْمَهْدِيُّ مِنِّي، أَجْلَى الْجَبْهَةِ، أَقْنَى الأَنْفِ

"The Mahdi is from me, with a broad forehead and an aquiline nose. He will fill the earth with justice."

Jami' at-Tirmidhi

Abwab al-Fitan

Jami' at-Tirmidhi #2244

يَقْتُلُ ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ الدَّجَّالَ بِبَابِ لُدَّ

"Isa ibn Maryam will slay the Dajjal at the Gate of Ludd."

Protection Hadith

Reciting Surah Al-Kahf

The Prophet ﷺ instructed believers to memorize and recite the first 10 verses of Surah Al-Kahf as an unshakeable spiritual shield against Dajjal's trial.
