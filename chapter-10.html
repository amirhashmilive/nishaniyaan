🟢 Sahih Bukhari & Sahih Muslim 🟡 Sunan Collections

Sahih al-Bukhari

Kitab al-Fitan (Book of Trials)

🟢 Sahih al-Bukhari #7118 & #7128

لاَ تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى تَخْرُجَ نَارٌ مِنْ أَرْضِ الْحِجَازِ

"The Hour will not come until a fire emerges from the land of the Hijaz illuminating the necks of camels in Busra."

Rigorous Selection:

Imam Bukhari compiled narrations with uninterrupted chains (*Muttasil*) of flawless narrators, detailing Dajjal's physical signs and early minor signs.

Sahih Muslim

Kitab al-Fitan wa Ashrat as-Sa'ah

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937 (Hadith of An-Nawwas ibn Sam'an)

مَا بَيْنَ خَلْقِ آدَمَ إِلَى قِيَامِ السَّاعَةِ خَلْقٌ أَكْبَرُ مِنَ الدَّجَّالِ

"There is no creation between the creation of Adam and the onset of the Hour greater than Dajjal."

Comprehensive Chronology:

Sahih Muslim provides the most comprehensive sequential narrations for the 40 days of Dajjal, the Mahdi, and the descent of Isa (AS).

Sunan Abi Dawud

Kitab al-Malahim (Book of Battles)

🟢 Sunan Abi Dawud #4285 & #4294

الْمَهْدِيُّ مِنِّي، أَجْلَى الْجَبْهَةِ، أَقْنَى الأَنْفِ

"The Mahdi is from me, with a broad forehead and an aquiline nose. He will fill the earth with justice as it was filled with tyranny."
