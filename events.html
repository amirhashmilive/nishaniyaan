Historical Events & Contemporary Observations

Events are rigorously classified into verified historical occurrences or contemporary observations.

Historical Event — 656 AH

The Fire of the Hijaz (Near Madinah)

Documented by Imam Al-Qurtubi and An-Nawawi: A massive volcanic eruption in Madinah lit the skies such that camels in Busra (Syria) were illuminated, fulfilling Sahih Bukhari #7118.

Contemporary Observation — 21st Century

High-Speed Global Telecommunications

Scholars note that modern instant voice and video broadcasting fulfill the observational criteria for distance-collapsing communication.
