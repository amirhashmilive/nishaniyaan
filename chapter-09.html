🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) Primary Authority

Surah 75

Surah Al-Qiyamah — The Resurrection

🟢 Surah Al-Qiyamah (75:1-4, 22-23)

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ ﴿١﴾ وَلَا أُقْسِمُ بِالنَّفْسِ اللَّوَّامَةِ ﴿٢﴾ أَيَحْسَبُ الْإِنسَانُ أَلَّن نَّجْمَعَ عِظَامَهُ ﴿٣﴾ بَلَىٰ قَادِرِينَ عَلَىٰ أَن نُّسَوِّيَ بَنَانَهُ ﴿٤﴾

"I swear by the Day of Resurrection, and I swear by the self-reproaching soul. Does man think that We will not assemble his bones? Yes, We are Able even to proportion his fingertips."

Surah 99

Surah Al-Zalzalah — The Earthquake

🟢 Surah Al-Zalzalah (99:1-3, 7-8)

إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا ﴿١﴾ وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا ﴿٢﴾

"When the earth is shaken with its final earthquake, and the earth discharges its burdens... So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see it, and whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see it."

Surah 101

Surah Al-Qariah — The Striking Calamity

🟢 Surah Al-Qariah (101:1-7)

الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿١﴾ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿٢﴾ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿٣﴾ يَوْمَ يَكُونُ النَّاسُ كَالْفَرَاشِ الْمَبْثُوثِ ﴿٤﴾

"The Striking Calamity — What is the Striking Calamity? It is the Day when people will be like moths dispersed, and mountains like carded wool."

Surah 18

Surah Al-Kahf — Protection Shield

🟢 Surah Al-Kahf (18:1-4) 🟢 Sahih Muslim Protection

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ الْكِتَابَ وَلَمْ يَجْعَل لَّهُ عِوَجًا ﴿١﴾

"Praise be to Allah Who has sent down upon His Servant the Book and has not made therein any deviance..."

Protection Focus:

Reciting the first 10 verses grants believers spiritual immunity against the psychological and material deceptions of Dajjal.
