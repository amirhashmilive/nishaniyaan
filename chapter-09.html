Surah 75

Surah Al-Qiyamah — The Resurrection

Surah Al-Qiyamah (75:1-4)

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ ﴿١﴾ وَلَا أُقْسِمُ بِالنَّفْسِ اللَّوَّامَةِ ﴿٢﴾

"I swear by the Day of Resurrection, and I swear by the self-reproaching soul..."

Surah 99

Surah Al-Zalzalah — The Earthquake

Surah Al-Zalzalah (99:1-3)

إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا ﴿١﴾ وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا ﴿٢﴾

"When the earth is shaken with its final earthquake, and the earth discharges its burdens..."

Surah 101

Surah Al-Qariah — The Striking Calamity

Surah Al-Qariah (101:1-5)

الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿١﴾ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿٢﴾ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ﴿٣﴾

"The Striking Calamity — What is the Striking Calamity? It is the Day people will be like moths dispersed..."

Surah 18

Surah Al-Kahf — Protection from Dajjal

Surah Al-Kahf (18:1-3)

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ الْكِتَابَ وَلَمْ يَجْعَل لَّهُ عِوَجًا ﴿١﴾

"Praise be to Allah Who revealed to His servant the Book and allowed no crookedness in it..."
