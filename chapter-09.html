Methodology Protocol

Criteria for Eschatological Claims

Determines why weak (*Da'if*) narrations cannot be used to establish definitive end-times predictions or specific calendar dates. Every report must have an unbroken chain (*Isnad*) of trustworthy narrators and text (*Matn*) free of severe anomalies.

Chapter 09 — Hermeneutics

Distinguishing Text from Interpretation

1. Sacred Text Primacy

Divine revelation remains eternal and unalterable. No modern event may force a reinterpretation of explicit text.

2. Observational Labeling

Contemporary social or natural phenomena are categorized strictly as observations — never dogmatically declared as fulfilled prophecy.
