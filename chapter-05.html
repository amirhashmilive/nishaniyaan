Phase 4 — Righteous Caliphate

Phase 4: Arrival of Imam Al-Mahdi

The emergence of Al-Mahdi ("The Guided One"), a righteous caliph from the household of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, who unifies the believers immediately preceding Isa's (AS) descent.

🟢 Sahih Sunan Verified

Phase 4 — Identity & Lineage

Who is Imam Mahdi? Prophetic Marks

🟢 Sunan Abu Dawud #4282

Name & Lineage

His name will match the Prophet's name (Muhammad ibn Abdullah) from the direct lineage of Fatimah (RA). He possesses a broad forehead and an aquiline nose. Allah will rectify him in a single night.

Phase 4 — Makkah Allegiance

Emergence & The Army Sunk in the Desert

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2882

Believers will give him pledge of allegiance (*Bay'ah*) between the Corner (*Rukn*) and the Station of Ibrahim (*Maqam*) in Makkah. An invading army sent to destroy him will be swallowed up by the earth in the desert of Al-Bayda.

Phase 4 — Era of Equity

His Rule: Filling Earth with Justice

🟢 Sunan Abu Dawud ⚪ Tafsir at-Tabari & Durr al-Manthur

"He will fill the earth with equity and justice just as it was previously filled with oppression, ruling 7 to 9 years globally (totaling up to 20 years)." Supported by black banners from Khurasan commanded by Shu'ayb ibn Salih al-Hashimi, and a combined naval force of 1,700 fleets (*Safina*) during the liberation of Bait al-Maqdis.

Phase 4 — Preparation for Isa (AS)

His Mission: Unifying Believers & Welcoming Isa

🟢 Sahih Muslim #156

Imam Mahdi leads the Muslim forces in Damascus. When Fajr prayer is called, Prophet Isa (AS) descends. The Mahdi offers Isa to lead the prayer, but Isa places his hand on his shoulders, saying: "No, your leader is from among you, an honor from Allah for this Ummah."
