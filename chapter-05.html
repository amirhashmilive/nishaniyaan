Phase 4 — Righteous Leadership

Phase 4: Arrival of Imam Mahdi

The righteous leader from the lineage of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ who emerges to unite believers during widespread turmoil.

Phase 4 — Lineage & Attributes

Who is Imam Mahdi?

Sunan Abi Dawud & Tirmidhi

Lineage of Fatimah (RA)

His name is Muhammad ibn Abdillah, matching the Prophet's ﷺ name. He has a broad forehead and aquiline nose, filling the earth with equity after oppression.

Phase 3 to 4 Transition

Emergence from Madinah to Makkah

Sunan Abi Dawud #4286

Allegiance at the Ka'bah

He travels from Madinah to Makkah where believers swear allegiance (*Bay'ah*) to him between the Corner (*Rukn*) and the Station of Ibrahim (*Maqam*). An army sent against him is swallowed by the earth at Al-Bayda.

Phase 4 — Era of Justice

His Rule: Justice & Abundance

Sunan Ibn Majah

Distribution of Wealth & Agricultural Bounty

During his seven-year rule, skies pour rain, earth yields crops in abundance, and wealth is distributed freely without counting.

His Mission: Uniting Against Tyranny

Imam Mahdi gathers the believer army at Jerusalem (*Bayt al-Maqdis*), preparing to lead morning prayer when Prophet Isa (AS) descends.
