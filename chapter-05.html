Phase 4 — Righteous Leadership

Phase 4: Arrival of Imam Mahdi

Imam Mahdi is a righteous leader from the lineage of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ who emerges during global turmoil to unite believers and establish justice.

🟢 Sahih Hadith Verified

Phase 4 — Lineage & Attributes

Who is Imam Mahdi? Name & Description

🟢 Sunan Abi Dawud #4285

Muhammad ibn Abdullah (Lineage of Fatimah RA)

His name matches the Prophet's ﷺ name, and his father's name matches the Prophet's father. He has a broad forehead and aquiline nose, filling the earth with justice as it was filled with tyranny.

Phase 4 — Allegiance at the Ka'bah

Emergence & Swallowing of Hostile Army

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2882

Allegiance Between Rukn & Maqam

He flees from Madinah to Makkah where believers recognize him and pledge allegiance (*Bay'ah*). An army dispatched to destroy him is swallowed by the earth at Al-Bayda desert.

Phase 4 — 7-Year Reign

His Rule: Abundance & Equity

🟡 Sunan Ibn Majah #4083

Agricultural & Financial Prosperity

During his 7-to-9 year rule, rain falls in abundance, crops sprout richly, livestock multiplies, and wealth is distributed freely without counting.

🟢 Sahih Muslim

His Mission: Leading Believers & Welcoming Isa (AS)

Imam Mahdi gathers the Muslim forces in Jerusalem (*Bayt al-Maqdis*). As he steps forward to lead the morning prayer (*Fajr*), Prophet Isa (AS) descends from heaven to join the congregation.
