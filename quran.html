Qur'an & End Times Revelations

Direct Quranic verses concerning the Hour (*As-Sa'ah*), Resurrection (*Al-Qiyamah*), and Escatology without unverified additions.

Surah Al-Qamar (54:1-2) Meccan Surah
اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ ﴿١﴾ وَإِن يَرَوْا آيَةً يُعْرِضُوا وَيَقُولُوا سِحْرٌ مُّسْتَمِرٌّ ﴿٢﴾

"The Hour has drawn near, and the moon has been split. And if they see a sign, they turn away and say, 'This is continuous magic.'"

Classical Tafsir (Ibn Kathir):

This occurred during the lifetime of Allah's Messenger ﷺ when the Meccans asked for a sign, and Allah split the moon into two distinct parts visible over Mount Abu Qubays.
