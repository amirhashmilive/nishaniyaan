Phase 3 — Central Eschatological Trial

Phase 3: The 439 Days of Al-Masih ad-Dajjal

Al-Masih ad-Dajjal (The False Messiah) is the greatest trial (*Fitnah*) in human history. He precedes the true Messiah, Prophet Isa (AS), attempting to deceive humanity through supernatural illusions.

🟢 Sahih Bukhari & Sahih Muslim Verified

Phase 3 — Identity & Physical Signs

Who is Dajjal? Prophetic Description

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #7128 🟢 Sahih Muslim #2933

Anatomy of the Deceiver

  • Right Eye Defective (*A'war*): Blind right eye protruding like a swollen, floating grape.
  • Complexion & Hair: Reddish-white skin tone with short, thick, curly hair.
  • Inscribed Mark: The Arabic letters ك ف ر (K-F-R: Disbeliever) clearly stamped between his eyes, legible to every believer regardless of literacy.
  • Evolving Claims: Begins claiming righteousness, then claims to be the Messiah, and finally claims Lordship/Divinity.

Phase 3 — Illusions & Supernatural Feats

The Great Deception & Miracles

🟢 Sahih Muslim

Fake Paradise & Hellfire Rivers

He brings two flowing rivers: his "paradise" is actually scorching fire, while his "hellfire" is cool, refreshing water.

🟢 Sahih Muslim

Commanding Rain & Vegetation

He commands the sky to rain and earth to sprout crops for towns that accept him, leaving rejecting towns in total famine.

🟢 Sahih Bukhari

Summoning Earthly Treasures

He passes through ruins and commands their buried wealth to follow him like swarms of bees.

🟢 Sahih Muslim

Apparent Resurrection Illusions

Devils take the form of deceased parents, speaking to offspring to convince them of Dajjal's false divinity.

Phase 3 — Geographic Origin & Speed

Where Dajjal Appears & Travel Speed

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Passageway Between Sham & Iraq

He emerges from a mountain pass between Syria and Iraq (*Khurasan* / Isfahan regional axis), moving across the earth with extreme velocity — described by the Prophet ﷺ like a storm cloud driven by wind, mounted upon an aerial craft with a 30-foot wingspan span between its ears (*Himar* symbolic metaphor).

Phase 3 — Adherents

His Followers & Believers

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2944

70,000 From Isfahan & The Materially Deceived

Dajjal will be followed by 70,000 from Isfahan wearing Persian shawls (*Tayalisa*), hypocrites, and those whose weak faith succumbs to economic desperation during the great famine.

Phase 3 — Divine Protection

Makkah & Madinah Guarded by Angels

🟢 Sahih Bukhari #1881

Barred Entry Across 7 Guarded Gates

Dajjal will traverse every land except Makkah and Madinah, each guarded by angels stationed at 7 distinct entry gates with drawn swords. Madinah will quake three times, expelling every hypocrite to join Dajjal's ranks outside.

Phase 3 — Spiritual Shield

Protection from Dajjal: Surah Al-Kahf

🟢 Sahih Muslim #809 🟢 Surah Al-Kahf

Recitation of the First 10 Verses

"Whoever memorizes ten verses from the beginning of Surah Al-Kahf will be protected from the trial of Dajjal."

Additional Protective Measures:

1. Seeking refuge in Allah during daily Tashahhud prayers.
2. Distance & fleeing from Dajjal's presence.
3. Firm grounding in Islamic Tawhid and Hadith descriptions.

Phase 3 — Qur'anic Example

The Cave Youths (*Ashab al-Kahf*)

🟢 Surah Al-Kahf (18:10-14)

Faith, Patience & Reliance on Allah

The young believers fled state tyranny to a cave, praying: "Our Lord, grant us from Yourself mercy and prepare for us from our affair right guidance." Their steadfastness exemplifies how believers withstand Dajjal's pressure.
