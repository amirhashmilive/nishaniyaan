Chapter 04 — Faith & Knowledge

Removal of Religious Knowledge

SIGN-MIN-002 · Sahih

The Death of Scholars

Allah will not take away knowledge by snatching it from hearts, but by the death of true scholars, until none remain and people follow unlearned leaders who issue misguided rulings.

Chapter 04 — Moral Collapse

Normalization of the Forbidden

SIGN-MIN-010 · Sahih Bukhari

Permissibility of Intoxicants & Immorality

People among the Ummah will consider permissible: adultery, silk for men, alcohol, and musical instruments — rebranding divine prohibitions under modern labels.
