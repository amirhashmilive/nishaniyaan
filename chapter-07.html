Phase 6 — Post-Dajjal Era

Phase 6: After Death of Dajjal

The release of Yajuj & Majuj, divine deliverance atop Mount Tur, and the subsequent global Golden Age of peace and abundance.

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an 21:96) 🟢 Sahih Muslim Verified

Phase 6 — The Swarming Hordes

Yajuj & Majuj (Gog and Magog) Released

🟢 Surah Al-Anbiya (21:96)

Breaching Dhul-Qarnayn's Barrier

Allah instructs Isa (AS) to lead believers to Mount Tur as Yajuj & Majuj swarm down from every elevation, drinking the Sea of Galilee entirely dry due to their vast numbers.

Phase 6 — Divine Deliverance

Destruction of Yajuj & Majuj

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Supplication & Parasitic Cleansing (*Naghfad*)

Isa (AS) supplicates to Allah, Who sends parasitic worms (*Naghfad*) into their necks, destroying them overnight. Giant birds carry their remains away, and rain cleanses the earth.

Phase 6 — Harmony of Nature

Global Peace & Ecological Reconciliation

🟢 Musnad Ahmad #9270

Taming of Predatory Beasts

Poisons are removed from venomous creatures; toddlers play with vipers unharmed, lions graze alongside cattle, and wolf and sheep dwell together in absolute safety.

🟢 Sunan Abi Dawud #4324

The Golden Age Under Isa (AS)

Isa (AS) lives on earth for 40 peaceful years, performs Hajj and Umrah, marries, and eventually passes away. Believers offer his Janazah and bury him in Madinah beside Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
