Chapter 07 — Verified Event

The Fire of the Hijaz — 654 AH

🔵 Historical Record · Sahih Bukhari #7118

Volcanic Eruption Near Madinah

Documented by Imam al-Qurtubi and an-Nawawi: a volcanic eruption in Madinah lit the skies so brightly that the necks of camels in Busra (Syria) were illuminated — fulfilling the Prophetic narration of a fire emerging from the Hijaz.

Global Digital Communications

🟣 Contemporary Observation

Distance-Collapsing Networks
