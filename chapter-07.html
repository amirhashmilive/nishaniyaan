Phase 6 — Global Reconciliation

Phase 6: After Dajjal — The Golden Age

The release and divine destruction of Yajuj & Majuj (Gog & Magog), followed by an unprecedented 40-year Golden Age of peace, security, and global spiritual unity.

🟢 Sahih Muslim Verified

Phase 6 — Unstoppable Horde

Yajuj & Majuj (Gog & Magog) Released

🟢 Surah Al-Anbiya (21:96) 🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Allah commands Isa (AS) to lead the believers to Mount Tur. Yajuj and Majuj swarm forth from every elevation in overwhelming numbers, drinking the Sea of Galilee (*Tiberias*) completely dry.

Phase 6 — Supplication & Cleansing

Supplication & Destruction of Yajuj & Majuj

🟢 Sahih Muslim #2937

Isa (AS) and his companions beseech Allah. Allah sends parasitic worms (*Naghaf*) into their necks, causing them to perish simultaneously. Giant birds carrying their bodies away, followed by torrential rain purifying the earth like a mirror.

Phase 6 — Harmony of Creation

World in Complete Peace & Harmony

🟢 Musnad Ahmad

Hostility, hatred, and jealousy vanish. Earth produces its fruits in abundance (a single pomegranate feeds a group). Lions graze with camels, leopards with cattle, and a young child plays safely with snakes.

Phase 6 — The 40-Year Reign

The 40-Year Reign & Demise of Isa (AS)

🟢 Sunan Abu Dawud #4324

Isa (AS) remains on earth for 40 years as a ruler and judge. He performs Hajj/Umrah. Upon his passing, the Muslims offer funeral prayers (*Janazah*) over him and bury him beside Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in Madinah.
