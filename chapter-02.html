Chapter 02 — The Moon Splitting

Surah Al-Qamar (54:1-2)

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) Meccan Surah

اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ ﴿١﴾ وَإِن يَرَوْا آيَةً يُعْرِضُوا وَيَقُولُوا سِحْرٌ مُّسْتَمِرٌّ ﴿٢﴾

"The Hour has drawn near, and the moon has been split. And if they see a sign, they turn away and say, 'This is continuous magic.'"

Tafsir Ibn Kathir:

Occurred during the lifetime of the Prophet ﷺ when the Meccans asked for a sign. Allah split the moon into two distinct parts visible over Mount Abu Qubays.

Chapter 02 — Gog & Magog

Surah Al-Anbiya (21:96-97)

🟢 Authentic (Qur'an) Major Sign Reference

حَتَّىٰ إِذَا فُتِحَتْ يَأْجُوجُ وَمَأْجُوجُ وَهُم مِّن كُلِّ حَدَبٍ يَنسِلُونَ ﴿٩٦﴾

"Until when Gog and Magog are let loose, and they swiftly swarm down from every elevation..."

Cross-Reference:

Linked to Major Sign #04. The wall built by Dhul-Qarnayn of iron and copper will be breached near the end of times (Surah Al-Kahf 18:94-98).
