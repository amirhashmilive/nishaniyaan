Phase 1 — Long Precursor Era

Phase 1: Before Dajjal (Minor Signs)

The gradual accumulation of Minor Signs setting the historical and social backdrop for Dajjal's emergence.

Phase 1 — Dimension 01

Faith & Belief (Loss of Knowledge)

SIGN-MIN-002 · Sahih Bukhari

Removal of Religious Erudition & False Claimants

Religious knowledge is taken away through the death of true scholars, giving rise to ~30 impostors (*Dajjalun*) claiming prophethood.

Phase 1 — Dimension 02

Morality & Social Normalization

SIGN-MIN-010 · Sahih Bukhari

Normalization of Intoxicants, Riba & Immorality

Widespread acceptance of forbidden acts (*Zina*, alcohol, usury) under newly invented names and euphemisms.

Phase 1 — Dimension 03

Technology, Speed & Media

SIGN-MIN-024 · Contemporary Observation

Time Acceleration & Distance Collapse

Rapid passage of time (*Taqarub al-Zaman*) and instant global broadcasting collapsing physical separation.

Phase 1 — Dimension 04

Natural Disasters & Anomalies

SIGN-MIN-030 · Sunan Tirmidhi

Frequent Earthquakes & Climate Anomalies

Increased frequency of seismic events, unpredictable weather patterns, and flooding across populated centers.

Phase 1 — Dimension 05

Politics, Conflict & Instability

SIGN-MIN-038 · Sahih Muslim

Nations Summoning Each Other Against the Ummah

Foreign powers inviting one another to devour the resources of Muslims like diners gathering around a bowl.

Phase 1 — Dimension 06

Economy, Inflation & Skyscrapers

SIGN-MIN-045 · Sahih Bukhari

Competition in Erecting Tall Buildings

Barefoot, destitute desert herders competing in constructing soaring skyscrapers amidst economic inflation.

Phase 1 — Dimension 07

Environmental Reversion

SIGN-MIN-050 · Sahih Muslim

Arabia Returning to Greenery & Rivers

The arid deserts of the Arabian Peninsula transforming back into green meadows and flowing rivers.

Phase 1 Summary: Where We Stand Today

The vast majority of Phase 1 Minor Signs have either manifested or are actively unfolding in contemporary society, setting the stage for Phase 2 near precursors.
