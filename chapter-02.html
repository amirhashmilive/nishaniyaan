Surah Al-Qamar & The Moon Splitting

Surah Al-Qamar (54:1-2) Meccan Surah
اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ ﴿١﴾ وَإِن يَرَوْا آيَةً يُعْرِضُوا وَيَقُولُوا سِحْرٌ مُّسْتَمِرٌّ ﴿٢﴾

"The Hour has drawn near, and the moon has been split. And if they see a sign, they turn away and say, 'This is continuous magic.'"

Tafsir Ibn Kathir:

Occurred during the lifetime of Allah's Messenger ﷺ when the Meccans asked for a sign, and Allah split the moon into two distinct parts visible over Mount Abu Qubays.

Surah Al-Anbiya & Yajuj wa Majuj

Surah Al-Anbiya (21:96-97) Major Sign Reference
حَتَّىٰ إِذَا فُتِحَتْ يَأْجُوجُ وَمَأْجُوجُ وَهُم مِّن كُلِّ حَدَبٍ يَنسِلُونَ ﴿٩٦﴾ وَاقْتَرَبَ الْوَعْدُ الْحَقُّ...

"Until when Gog and Magog are let loose, and they swiftly swarm down from every elevation. And the true promise draws near..."

Cross-Reference:

Linked to Major Sign #04 and Sahih Muslim Hadith #2937 regarding the wall constructed of iron and copper by Dhul-Qarnayn.
